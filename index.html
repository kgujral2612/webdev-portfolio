About

Kaushambi is an accomplished master's student at Portland State University, possessing more than 3 years of experience as a Software Engineer. Her expertise lies in developing reliable applications complemented by a proven track record of collaboration and adaptability.

Her professional journey began at Dell Technologies, where she had the chance to collaborate with global teams to aid in the making of a micro-service architecture for dell.com, and developing a new application for dell.com's Deals Web Pages. As a curious explorer in the world of Artificial Intelligence, she is pursuing MS in CS with a focus on AI/ML.

Her adaptability shines through working on her very own podcast called NLP Pulse, tutoring undergrad CS students, mentoring new, incoming International Students, and being an ICSP scholarship recepient all while maintaining a 3.97 GPA.

After a busy day, she unwinds by practicing Yoga, reading self-help books, or watching a good movie.
