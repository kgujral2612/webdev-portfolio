About me

I am a Software Engineer with an interest in everything latest in tech. I believe in multi-disciplinary learning and enjoy dabbling with a variety of tech & software.

My professional journey began at Dell Technologies, where I had the chance to collaborate with global teams to aid in the making of a micro-service architecture for dell.com, and developing a new application for dell.com's Deals Web Pages.

As a curious explorer in the world of Artificial Intelligence, I am pursuing MS in CS at Portland State University with a focus on AI/ML. During this time, I have had the opportunity to not only improve my software development knowledge but also learn more about Quantum Computing, Machine Learning, and Natural Language Processing.

I like to stay involved- I've worked as an international student mentor, I am a scholarship recepient and cultural presentor at the International Cultural Service Program, and I'm currently working as a CS tutor providing guidance to undergrad CS students.

My latest venture is my own podcast titled NLP Pulse that focuses on the latest developments in the field of Natural Language Processing.
